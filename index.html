About me...

I am an EIT certified mechanical engineer with a passion for robotics and software. My education includes advanced mathematics and control system design. Professionally, I have experience designing medical devices for minimally invasive surgery and managing government-funded energy efficiency projects. Now I would like to move my career more firmly into the technology sector. To that end, I have recently relocated to beautiful Santa Barbara, CA to pursue a degree in Computer Science. In my free time I'm working toward a full-stack web development certificate from freecodecamp.com. Going forward, I would like to integrate my web skills with robots and the sensors in the "Internet of Things."
